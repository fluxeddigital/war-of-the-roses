The Course

One of the finest golf clubs in Yorkshire with a quite stunning setting next to the meandering River Wharfe. A fabulous test of golf and a perfect location for this matchplay classic. Ilkley Golf Club is no stranger to Ryder Cups, boasting Colin Montgomerie and Mark James as honorary life members.
